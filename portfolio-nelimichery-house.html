ViNi's Lakeview Guest House

Architecture and Interior design for ViNi's Lakeview Guest House, Nemilicherry

Vini's Lakeview is a guesthouse made for nature and pet lovers. The space was aimed to be rented as an Airbnb or converted into a house later. The design idea was to create two structures with a central courtyard space that connects the two blocks with a garden and landscape on the sides. The structure overlooks the lake and the backyard swimming pool creates a very calm balcony space to chill.
