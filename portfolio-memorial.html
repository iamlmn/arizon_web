Sp.Nallama Naidu Memorial design

Architecture design for Sp.Nallama Naidu Memorial, Theni

The memorial is located in the farmlands that where owned by late Sp. Nallama Naidu sir. The design idea was to create a structure of order in a chaotic landscape that protrudes from land signifying 'Order in Chaos'. The landscape is extruded in angles and the central structure is rigid and airy with rectangular jaalis.There is a cutout on the roof with a symbolic tree represnting the respected late SP. Nallama Naidu sir.
